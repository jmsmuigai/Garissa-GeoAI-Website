GeoAI for a Resilient Garissa

Transforming crisis response from reactive to predictive through intelligent, data-driven insights.

The Challenge

Historically, crises were managed through delayed phone reports and manual mapping, leading to slow response times and an escalation of emergencies like cholera outbreaks.

The Vision

To build a connected, intelligent "system of systems"—the ASAL Integrated Resource Management Platform—that centralizes data and uses AI to generate predictive, life-saving insights.

How It Works

Our GeoAI workflow transforms data from multiple sources into actionable intelligence, enabling dynamic mapping, AI-powered analytics, and automated alerts for frontline decision-makers.

Key Benefits

Achieve faster response times, enable proactive risk management, optimize resource allocation, and ultimately build more resilient and safer communities throughout Garissa County.
